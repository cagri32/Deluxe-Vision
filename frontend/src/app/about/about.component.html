About Deluxe Vision

Deluxe Vision is a website created for a school project by Ahmet Ozer and Shahram Nasir. Our goal was to create an online shopping platform with a modern and responsive design.

We designed this website using Angular and Angular Material for the front-end, and Firebase for the back-end. Our vision was to create a seamless shopping experience for our users, with an easy-to-use interface and efficient database management.

We hope you enjoy using Deluxe Vision as much as we enjoyed creating it!

Our Team

Ahmet and Shahram are both computer science students at York University. They both have a passion for web development and worked together to create Deluxe Vision for their school project. Shahram is responsible for the front-end development and design, while Ahmet focused on the back-end and database management.

Future Plans

We plan on expanding Deluxe Vision to include more products and categories. We also want to implement features such as user reviews and ratings, and a recommendation engine. Our goal is to create a platform that provides a personalized shopping experience for each user.
